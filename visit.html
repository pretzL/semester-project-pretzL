Location

Address: Museumsveien 3, 4001 Stavanger

The museum is conveniently located in the heart of Stavanger. You can take any bus going to Stavanger Sentrum from anywhere, and walk to the museum. Bus X31 also stops right outside of the museum.

Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organize a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour using this form.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessible ramps . It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

If you have any other concerns, you are more than welcome to contact us via this form.
